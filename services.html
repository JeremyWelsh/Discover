All tours run on the time schedule of 9AM, 12PM, 3PM, or 6PM and start at Midland Park on Lambton Quay. Each tour can accomodate for up to 15 people and go for 2 hours.

Makara Peak Windmills

Visit the coast on an electric mini-bus to get up close and personal with the local windfarm Wellington has to offer! There are 62 windmills scattered across the hills of Makara with one placed one the walking path for you to understand the shear scale of the turbines. The windfarm was officially completed back in 2009. Along with the windmills, you will have a spectacular view of the ocean, and the sheep that the windmills share the farm with.

Botanical Wander

Explore the 250,000 square metre Wellington Botanic Gardens filled with unique landscapes and colourful flower arrangements. On the walk you will visit the turreted gazebo next to the duck pond, the Lady Norwood Rose Garden, the Picnic Cafe, and possibly see glow worms if you are lucky enough! Along with these activities, you may also choose to visit the Space Place Planetarium, to learn about astronomy, or take the Cable Car for a vibrant trip down to Lambton Quay.

Sports Deluxe

Get an in depth tour of Sky Stadium, visiting the corporate boxes, the tunnel leading onto the field, the cricket training nets and more. Depending on the booking day, you may get an inside look into the training routines of the teams playing that night. After the tour of the aptly named Cake Tin, the tour continues on towards the Basin Reserve. While you are there, you will be able visit the cricket museum, view history of Cricket Wellington in the changing rooms and the magnificent pitch from the RA Vance Stand.

Round The Bays

On this tour you will get to experience the local Crocodile Bikes starting the ride beside Waitangi Park. From there you will visit Oriental Bay where you could grab an icecream to cool down on a perfect Wellington day. After a quick stop at Oriental Bay, we will continue on Evans Bay Parade towards the Zephyrometer otherwise know as the Wind Needle. After the scenic ride around the bays to drop the bikes back off, the tour will take you up Mount Victoria for a picturesque view of Wellington.
