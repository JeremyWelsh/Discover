Discover Welly

Discover Welly is an enviromentally friendly guided tour company with 5 differnt tours to offer. The tours use multiple types of transport including walking, electric scootering, riding an electric minibus, and riding Crocodile Bikes. Discover Welly was founded in 2020 with our sights set on creating a sustainble way of allowing tourists to explore Wellinton.

Tours

Discover Welly has 5 tours that take you across all of Wellington. One tour takes you around the wonderful Botanical Gardens that Wellington has to offer. Other tours take you sight seeing around Wellington's compact city, with key destinations including: The Beehive, Sky Stadium, The Bucket Fountain, Oriental Bay, Mount Victoria and more. If you want to explore further outside of Wellington, you could take our tour to the Makara Peak Windfarm to get up close and personal with a full size wind turbine and view many others by the coast.

Discover Welly's mission

Our Goal for Discover Welly is to provide envirmentally sustainable tours in and around Wellinton. We aim to achieve this by using carbon neutral transport, like our electric scooters and minibus, or by providing walking or biking tours.
